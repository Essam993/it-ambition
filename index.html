About Us

Who are we ?

IT Ambition is your gateway to offshore your IT services overseas. We help our customers extend thier capability and win the competition. We mainly offshore the IT services from Germany and Europe to Egypt and Indian .IT ambition is founded in 2018. Its Head office is in Egypt and we have onsite teams in Germany and Indian.

Read more about us

We are not working only remotely with our customers but also support them in Germany by expertises . We can make immediate face to face meetings, on-site deployment, gathering requirements , immediate recovery in emergency and all other staffs that need physical existence

For many customers advicing about road map and suitable IT services is more important than implementing system. We provide free consulting services speiically for small business and free onsite visit inside germany

Our leading edge is quality. We are working by latest industry standers to provide reasonable level of quality for each project. Quality is the responsibility of everyone from development the application to the deployment in production. We mange our project by Agile methodology. customers are involved in every steps. We use continues integration and its tools to fix issues in early stages. We depend on automation test in all level from unit test to integration test to satisfy high level of code coverage and finally we have testing team that acts as end Users

IT Ambition services all size of IT services from stand alone freelancer to enterprise. We know our client needs and have suitable solution for their business

Egypt is located in the same timezone of most of Europa countries especially Germany and Indian has different one so we work around the clock for our clients business. This gives our clients a good chance if they support their customers around the clock
